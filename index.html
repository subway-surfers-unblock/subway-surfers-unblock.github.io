What is Subway Surfers?

Subway Surfers is a classic endless runner game where players control a graffiti-tagging teenager fleeing from a grumpy inspector and his dog. The gameplay revolves around dodging trains, jumping over obstacles, and collecting coins as you race through the subway tracks.

Release Date and Developer

The game was released on May 24, 2012, and was developed by Danish companies Kiloo and SYBO Games. Initially launched for iOS and Android platforms, it later expanded to Windows and web browsers.

How to Play Subway Surfers

Gameplay is simple yet challenging. The character runs automatically, and your job is to control movement using swipes or keys:
